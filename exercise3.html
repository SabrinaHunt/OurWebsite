    Recipe 1: The Classic Mojito

    Ingredients

    • 2 Ounces light rum
    • 2 Teaspoons sugar
    • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
    • 1 lime (cut into 2 halves)
    • Garnish: mint sprig
    • Small cup of Club Soda: mint sprig
    • Small tray of ice cubes

    Method

    1. Place the sugar, mint leaves, and a splash of club soda into a highball glass
    2. Muddle well to disolve the sugar and to release the flavour of the mint
    3. Squeeze the huice from both halves of a lime into the glass, dropping one-half into the glass
    4. Add the rum
    5. Stir well
    6. Fill the glass with ice cubes
    7. Top with club soda
    8. Garnish with a mint sprig
    Love it

    Recipe 3: Passion Fruit Martini

    Ingedients

    • 2 ripe passion fruits (they should have a crinkly appearance), halved
    • 60ml vanilla vodka
    • 30ml passoa
    • 1tbsp lime juice
    • 1tbsp sugar syrup
    • Prosecco to serve
    • Small tray of ice cubes

    Method

    1. Scoop the seeds from one of the passion fruits into the glass of a cocktail shaker
    2. Add the vodka, passoa, lime juice and sugar syrup into the glass
    3. Add a handful of ice and shake well
    4. strain into 2 martini glasses and top up with prosecco
    5. Add half a passion fruit to each. Serve immediately


      6. Love it

    Recipe 4: Bloody Mary

    Ingredients

    • 1/4 cup of tomatoe juice
    • 2 tbsp vodka (any)
    • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 3/4 tsp freshly grated horseradish
    • 3 dashes hot pepper sauce e.g Tabasco
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 1 dash of freshly groun black pepper
    • Small tray of ice cubes
    • 1/4 tsp fresh lemon juice
    • 1 stalk celery
    • 1 lemon wedge (optional)

    Method

    1. In a 11 ounce highball glass, stir together the tomato juice, vodka, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, hot sauce, salt and pepper
    2. Fill the glass with ice
    3. Pour the mixture into another glass - not the one containing ice
    4. Pour back and fourth between the glasses 3 to 4 times to mix weell
    5. Sprink lemon juice over
    6. Garnish with celery stalk and lemon wedge


      7. Love it
